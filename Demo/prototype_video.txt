# NetraAI – Prototype Demo Video Script

Purpose:
This file defines the concept, flow, and narration outline for NetraAI prototype demo videos.
It is intended for early-stage demos, GitHub previews, investor pitches, and vision communication.

---

## DEMO VIDEO OVERVIEW

Video Length: 60–120 seconds
Format: Conceptual prototype (no real hardware required)
Style: Clean, futuristic, human-focused

The demo shows how NetraAI AR Glasses assist a user in real life without distraction.

---

## SCENE 1 – INTRO (5–10 seconds)

Visual:
A person puts on normal-looking AR glasses.
No flashy UI, no heavy design.

Text / Voiceover:
"What if knowledge appeared exactly when you needed it?"

Purpose:
Introduce NetraAI as invisible, wearable intelligence.

---

## SCENE 2 – URBAN AR ASSISTANT

Visual:
User walking in a city.
Camera POV shows buildings, signboards, streets.

AR Overlay:

* Direction arrow
* Building name
* Distance indicator

Audio (Bone Conduction):
"Turn left in 20 meters. This building is the City Library."

System Flow (Behind the Scenes):
Camera → Vision Context → Intent Detection → AR + Audio

Purpose:
Demonstrate navigation and contextual awareness.

---

## SCENE 3 – LEARNING COMPANION MODE

Visual:
User looking at a machine, book, or object.

AR Overlay:

* Object name
* Short explanation (1–2 lines)

Audio:
"This is a DC motor. It converts electrical energy into mechanical energy."

Purpose:
Show learning without stopping work or opening a phone.

---

## SCENE 4 – COOKING ASSISTANT

Visual:
User in kitchen looking at vegetables and utensils.

AR Overlay:

* Ingredient names
* Step indicator

Audio:
"You can make vegetable stir fry. Step one: heat oil in the pan."

Purpose:
Show practical everyday assistance.

---

## SCENE 5 – PRIVACY & EDGE AI MOMENT

Visual:
Minimal animation showing:

* Device processing locally
* Lock icon on memory module

Text:
"Your data stays with you."
"No always-on cloud."

Purpose:
Highlight privacy-first architecture.

---

## SCENE 6 – HYBRID INTELLIGENCE

Visual:
Simple diagram animation:
Device ↔ Optional Cloud

Text:
"Offline when needed. Online when useful."

Purpose:
Explain hybrid AI model in simple terms.

---

## SCENE 7 – OUTRO / VISION

Visual:
User removes glasses.
Normal life continues.

Voiceover:
"NetraAI doesn’t replace human intelligence. It empowers it."

Text on Screen:
NetraAI – Empowering Humans with Seamless Access to Knowledge

---

## END NOTE

This prototype demo is conceptual.
It represents the vision, user experience, and system flow of NetraAI.

The goal is clarity, not complexity.
Technology should feel calm, helpful, and human.
